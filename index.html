Ashmore House B&B Cashel Town is the home of Brendan and Laura Ryan who have been welcoming guests for bed and breakfast accommodation for more than a decade

It has five tastefully decorated guest rooms, all are en-suite. Guests are offered an extensive breakfast menu. Breakfasts are freshly cooked to order and served in the spacious dining room.
